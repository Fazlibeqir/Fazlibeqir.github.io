Aniseed (lat. Pimpinella anisum)

Do you know see .... Anise in Albanian lands grows in a semi-wild state, otherwise it can be cultivated. Grows in sunny places, has white leaves, fruits are pear-shaped. Its fruits are used against asthma, cough, for appetite, against insomnia, hysteria, weakened stomach, lung diseases and against bloating.
