Plastic-eating bacteria
European researchers identified a new bacterium that feeds on polyurethane, a kind of plastic that is difficult to recycle or destroy.

Streptococcus pneumoniae, Diplococcus pneumoniae

Or simply pneumococcus are gram-positive diplococcal bacteria arranged in pairs or short strings, where their cells are lanceolate. The term in the form of lancets means that the diplococci are oval with a slightly sharp terminal part. When cultured in blood agar, they are alpha-hemolytic

Bacteriology

Is the branch and specialty of biology that studies the morphology, ecology, genetics and biochemistry of bacteria as well as many other aspects related to them.

The Seven Daughters of Eve: The Science That Reveals Our Genetic Ancestry

Is a 2001 book by Bryan Sykes that introduces the science of human mitochondrial genetics to a wide audience. Sykes explains the principles of genetics and human evolution, the features of mitochondrial DNA, and the analysis of ancient DNA to genetically link modern humans to prehistoric ancestors.
